

# NextDigits — AI Consultancy Landing Page

A single-page, modern website for NextDigits with a futuristic, AI-themed design featuring gradients, glows, and sleek typography. Inspired by the NxWave layout but with a distinct techy identity.

---

## Design Direction
- **Dark background** with vibrant gradient accents (blues, purples, cyans)
- Subtle glow effects and gradient borders on cards
- Modern sans-serif typography with bold headings
- Smooth scroll animations between sections
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop)

---

## Page Sections (Single Scrollable Page)

### 1. **Hero Section**
- Bold tagline: "AI Consultancy | Business Optimization | Innovation"
- Subheading describing NextDigits' mission
- Call-to-action button ("Get in Touch" / "Explore Our Services")
- Subtle animated gradient background or particle effect

### 2. **About / What We Do**
- Brief overview of NextDigits as an AI consultancy startup
- Key value propositions in 2-3 short cards (e.g., "AI-Driven Solutions", "Process Optimization", "Industry Expertise")

### 3. **AI / Data Science Services**
- Card grid showcasing services: Sentiment Analysis, Text Classification, NER, Translation, Summarization, Keyword Extraction, Document Clustering, Report Generation, Advanced Text Analytics, Data Cleaning for NLP, OCR Optimization, AI Model Evaluation, Prompt Engineering

### 4. **Trainings**
- Section highlighting training offerings: Agentic AI, Prompt Engineering, LLM Ops, RAG Systems, Alteryx

### 5. **AI Solution Architecture**
- Cards or list for: AI Model/LLM Integration, Evaluations, Optimization, Scalable Pipelines

### 6. **Intelligent Automation Services**
- Categories displayed as cards: Business Process Management, Development (RPA, AI, Low Code/Appian), Business Analysis, Agile/AI Product Management, Test Management, Solution Architecture

### 7. **Industry Expertise — Insurance**
- Focused section on insurance domain: Guidewire, AI-based Claims Automation, Document Understanding
- Process areas: Claims, Billing, Policy

### 8. **Contact / CTA Section**
- Simple call-to-action with email address or "Schedule a Consultation" prompt
- Social media links (placeholder)

### 9. **Footer**
- Company name, copyright, navigation links

---

## Key Features
- Smooth scroll navigation with a sticky header
- Animated section reveals on scroll
- Responsive card layouts for all service categories
- Clean, organized presentation of the extensive service catalog
- No backend required — purely static content
